5 Must-Have Motorcycle Accessories For Bikers


Are you one of the motorcycle enthusiasts who are always ready for a motorcycle road trip amid the alluring views and cityscape? If yes, this blog is surely for you. This blog penned by the experts of CSL, the most recommended motorcycle parts supplier in the Philippines, would let you know 5 must-have motorcycle accessories before you plan for your next adventurous road trip. The following 5 motorcycle accessories ensure you fundamental safety without even compromising with your style statement. And, here lies the greatest USP of these motorcycle accessories.


1. Helmet-


We know that no rider on this planet is unaware of helmets as a helmet is the most basic motorcycle accessory a rider should have. There are so many fashionable and funky helmets are available in the market. Get one for you that suits you and your motorcycle. But, we, being a reputed motorcycle parts supplier in the Philippines would like to recommend every rider to purchase a full-coverage helmet for the best protection of head and face.


2. Riding gloves-


A decent and good quality pair of riding gloves not only give someone a biker-boy look but also protects the fingers of the rider from sudden road accidents. Besides, on a road trip, when the hands get exposed to the sunlight, a pair of riding gloves protect the hands from the scorching sunlight and prevent heat.


3. Riding jacket and other body armor-


If you are wearing a premium quality riding jacket on your motorcycle road trip, your upper part of the body is almost fully secure. The heavily padded riding jackets along with other body armors can ensure the protection of the chest, waist, hands, elbows of the bike riders. A variety of riding jackets is available these days in the market. Such as weather-friendly riding jackets, airbag riding jackets, etc. You need to get one for yourself according to your preference.


4. Riding trousers-


Most people underestimate the importance of riding trousers. The lower part of a rider should be equally protected just like the upper part. A riding trouser is potential enough to protect the feet, thighs, and keens of a rider from accidental injuries. Along with protection, a good quality riding jacket provides the utmost comfort while riding for a longer period of time.


5. Riding goggles-


Wearing a riding goggle brings additional protection to the eyes of a rider. Because, there remain some possibilities of entering dust, dirt, and small flying debris to the eyes of the riders while riding a motorcycle, even if the rider has put on a helmet. Besides the protection, a riding goggle brings a cool and funky look to the whole riding outfit. For a safe ride, riders must emphasize these aforementioned 5 riding accessories. Hence, purchase good quality motorcycle accessories from reliable dealers only.
