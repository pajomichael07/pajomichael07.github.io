Frequently Asked Question

A quality battery charger or Battery Tender, tire pressure gauge, chain cleaner & lubricant, bike clearing supplies, and rear stands are the most popular 'must have' garage items.

Our suppliers require prepayment of special order items. You will receive a phone call the moment your special order is here.

The usual wait time is between 7-14 business days from date of purchase. Sometimes this wait time is longer due to international suppliers, customs, and backorders. We offer expedited shipping when applicable.

We have a few options: when looking for OEM parts, try the microfiche or you can always give us a call. This is the advantage of dealing with your local BMW, Ducati, and KTM dealership directly; a feature buying online doesn't usually offer. Our parts department staff are motorcycle experts and do everything they can to answer your question. If we don't immediately know the answer, we will find out.

We offer a 30-day return policy on unused, as-new items. Certain return exclusions apply such as electrical components, helmets, apparel, and all special order items as we cannot return them to the supplier. There is a no return or store credit for special orders, cancelled orders, items not picked up after 30 days or discounted/sale items sold below MSRP. If original packaging has been opened it cannot be returned.

Motolane PH always has some accessories available at discounted prices. Please ask the parts team what is available.

The answer is likely "Yes." Motolane is an authorized dealer for most brands in the motorcyle industry.

We are happy to arrange for shipping to your residence or business. However, we do not ship internationally.
